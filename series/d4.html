Story

How I Met Your Mother tells the story of Ted Mosby, an architect living in New York City, as he recounts to his children the long and complicated journey that led him to meet their mother.

The story begins in 2005, when Ted’s best friend Marshall announces that he plans to propose to his longtime girlfriend Lily. This event makes Ted realize that he also wants to find the love of his life, beginning a long and often hilarious search for “the one.”

Along the way, Ted spends most of his time with his close group of friends: the charming but extremely unpredictable Barney Stinson, the loyal and romantic Marshall Eriksen, the artistic and caring Lily Aldrin, and the independent journalist Robin Scherbatsky.

Each episode follows their adventures through relationships, careers, friendships, and the ups and downs of adult life in New York. The show mixes comedy, romance, and emotional moments while gradually revealing clues about the mysterious woman Ted will eventually marry.

Known for its unique storytelling style, running jokes, and memorable characters, How I Met Your Mother became one of the most popular sitcoms of the 2000s, leaving a lasting impact on modern television comedy.
