Story

Prison Break follows Michael Scofield, a brilliant structural engineer who devises an elaborate plan to break his wrongly convicted brother Lincoln Burrows out of Fox River State Penitentiary. Lincoln faces the death penalty for a crime he didn’t commit.

Michael intentionally gets himself imprisoned in order to help his brother escape. Using his intellect, tattoos containing hidden schematics, and careful planning, he navigates the dangerous prison environment while forming alliances and avoiding enemies.

The series explores the high-stakes tension of life inside and outside prison walls, including chases, conspiracies, and the influence of a shadowy organization behind Lincoln’s framing. Each season escalates the stakes as the characters face increasingly complex challenges and moral dilemmas.

Themes of loyalty, family, sacrifice, and clever problem-solving drive the story. With relentless pacing, clever plot twists, and memorable characters, Prison Break became one of the most intense and addictive drama series of the 2000s.
