Story

The Walking Dead follows a group of survivors navigating a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies, commonly referred to as walkers. After awakening from a coma, sheriff Rick Grimes discovers society has collapsed and must adapt to this new, dangerous world.

The survivors face constant threats from both the undead and other humans, as resources are scarce and moral boundaries blur. Leadership, trust, and survival are tested as the group journeys through abandoned towns, forests, and dangerous settlements.

The series explores themes of humanity, hope, loss, and the struggle to retain morality when civilization falls. Characters are forced to make impossible decisions while facing loss, betrayal, and the constant threat of death.

Known for its tense storytelling, complex characters, and graphic depiction of survival horror, The Walking Dead blends action, drama, and suspense, making it one of the most influential and longest-running zombie apocalypse series on television.
