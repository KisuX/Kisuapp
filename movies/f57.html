Story

The film tells the passionate love story of Asya, a village girl, and İlyas, a truck driver. They marry and dream of becoming a happy family with their young son, Samet, but İlyas’s struggles with alcohol and hardships shake their family life.

Soon, İlyas disappears, and Asya sets out on the road with Samet. During this journey, she meets a kind-hearted man named Cemşit, who helps both her and Samet, forming a strong bond between them.

Years later, İlyas unexpectedly returns, and Asya must choose between her past and present. The film explores deep emotions of love, sacrifice, and loyalty.
