Story

In a fantastical medieval world, a noblewoman named Cherry is left alone in a remote castle by her neglectful husband. When her husband makes a bet with his friend Manfred that he can seduce her within a hundred nights, Cherry’s devoted maid Hero steps in to protect her by telling captivating stories night after night. Over 100 nights, their bond deepens and challenges emerge, blending fantasy, romance and personal courage.

Based on the graphic novel *The One Hundred Nights of Hero*, the film reimagines themes of love, restraint and resistance in an imaginative fairy‑tale setting, exploring power dynamics, storytelling as a tool of survival, and unexpected emotional alliances between its characters.

This whimsical fantasy is directed and written by Julia Jackman, with lush visuals and a layered narrative rooted in mythic inspiration.
