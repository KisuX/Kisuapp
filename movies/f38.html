Story

Ten strangers, each with their own secrets, find themselves stranded at a remote desert motel during a storm. As the night progresses, they start being murdered one by one, and suspicion runs high among them. The survivors must unravel the mystery and figure out who is behind the killings before it’s too late.

Identity delves into themes of psychological tension, duality, and human nature. As the plot twists and hidden connections come to light, the true nature of the events is gradually revealed, keeping the audience on edge until the shocking conclusion.

With a claustrophobic setting, tight pacing, and a suspenseful storyline, Identity blends mystery and thriller elements to create an engaging and unpredictable cinematic experience.
