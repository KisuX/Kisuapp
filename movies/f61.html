Story

John and Jane Smith appear to be an ordinary married couple living a quiet suburban life. However, beneath their routine relationship lies a surprising secret. Both of them work as highly skilled assassins for different organizations, and neither knows about the other’s true profession.

Their lives take an unexpected turn when they are assigned to the same mission and discover each other’s hidden identities. What begins as a shocking revelation quickly turns into a dangerous situation, forcing them to confront both their employers and the reality of their marriage.

As chaos unfolds around them, John and Jane must decide whether they are enemies or partners. The story blends action, humor, and romance while exploring trust, secrets, and the complicated dynamics of a relationship built on deception.
