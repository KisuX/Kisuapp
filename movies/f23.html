Story

Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is wrongly convicted of murdering his wife and her lover. Sentenced to two consecutive life terms at Shawshank State Penitentiary, he forms a friendship with fellow inmate Ellis “Red” Redding, finding ways to retain hope and dignity amid the harsh realities of prison life.

The story explores themes of hope, resilience, friendship, and the human spirit. Through patience, ingenuity, and an unbreakable will, Andy impacts the lives of those around him while quietly planning a path to freedom.

Praised for its direction, performances, and emotional depth, The Shawshank Redemption is widely regarded as one of the greatest films of all time.
