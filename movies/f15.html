Story

Over a thousand years ago, a young boy named Bilal and his sister are taken from their home and forced into slavery. Despite the injustice they face, Bilal’s determination and brave spirit push him to rise above hardship. As he grows, Bilal finds his voice and stands up against greed and oppression, becoming a symbol of courage and freedom.

The animated adventure follows his journey from slave to respected leader, blending action, emotion, and historical elements in a richly detailed world. With sweeping landscapes, inspiring themes, and memorable characters, the film tells a story of resilience, hope, and the power of finding one’s voice.

Bilal’s story is inspired by true events and celebrates bravery in the face of overwhelming odds. Its animation and powerful narrative make it a compelling watch for audiences of all ages.
