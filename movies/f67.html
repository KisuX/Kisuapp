Story

In a war between humanity and the sentient computer network Skynet, resistance leader John Connor sends Kyle Reese back to 1984 to protect his mother Sarah Connor from an assassin Terminator. When Reese arrives, he discovers an altered timeline where events have changed drastically — Sarah is no longer a frightened waitress but a trained warrior protected by a reprogrammed Terminator, and the future is once again at stake.

Terminator Genisys blends action and science‑fiction with time‑travel twists, opening a parallel timeline that diverges from the original series mythology. The fight against Skynet leads to new alliances and threats as past and future collide.
