Story

This documentary‑style concert film presents never‑before‑seen footage, restored recordings and candid moments with Elvis Presley, showcasing his electrifying performances, charismatic stage presence, and personal reflections. It focuses especially on his 1970s Las Vegas residencies and national tours, weaving together concert sequences with Presley’s own narration and rare interviews.

Unlike a traditional biopic, EPiC blends archival material with dynamic editing to celebrate Elvis’s music and artistic legacy. The film aims to give fans an immersive experience of the King of Rock ’n’ Roll at the height of his performance powers, capturing both iconic shows and quieter, insightful moments.

Directed by Baz Luhrmann as a follow‑up to his 2022 *Elvis* biopic, the film debuted at the Toronto International Film Festival and released in IMAX and conventional theaters in early 2026.
