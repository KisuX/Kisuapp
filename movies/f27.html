Story

Maximus Decimus Meridius, a respected Roman general, is betrayed when the emperor’s corrupt son, Commodus, murders his family and seizes the throne. Reduced to slavery, Maximus rises as a gladiator to avenge his loved ones and restore honor.

The film explores themes of loyalty, vengeance, and the struggle between personal integrity and political corruption. Through epic battles and the brutal spectacle of the arena, Maximus becomes a symbol of courage and justice.

Starring Russell Crowe, Joaquin Phoenix, Connie Nielsen, and Oliver Reed, Gladiator won 5 Academy Awards including Best Picture and Best Actor, cementing its status as a modern classic.
