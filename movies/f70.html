Story

Jack Dawson, a struggling artist, wins a ticket aboard the RMS Titanic and meets Rose DeWitt Bukater, a young woman from a wealthy family engaged to another man. Despite social differences, Jack and Rose form a deep bond as the ship embarks on its fateful maiden voyage. Their love story unfolds against the backdrop of the ship's tragic sinking, exploring themes of class, freedom, and sacrifice.

The film combines historical events with a romantic narrative, featuring epic visuals, emotional performances, and a score that has become iconic. Titanic remains one of the highest-grossing films in history.
