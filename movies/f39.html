Story

Street meat vendor Kadir realizes that he can turn his long-held dream of flying into reality using a special device called a paramotor. With hope and determination, he takes parachute training to pursue his dream, but this unusual desire is met with curiosity and criticism from those around him. In the city’s political climate, his passion is even seen as a threat to airspace safety.

Despite all the pressures, Kadir refuses to give up his desire to soar through the sky with his colorful parachute and continues to chase his dream.

With humor and humanity, the film explores themes of ambition, perseverance, and conflict with society, delivering an inspiring and hopeful story to the audience.
