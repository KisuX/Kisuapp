Story

Detective William Somerset is a veteran investigator who is close to retirement. When a disturbing murder case appears, he is partnered with a young and ambitious detective named David Mills. Together they begin investigating a series of brutal crimes that shock the entire city.

As the investigation continues, the detectives realize the killer is following a disturbing pattern inspired by the seven deadly sins. Each crime becomes more complex and horrifying, pushing the detectives deeper into a dark and dangerous psychological game.

While racing against time, Somerset and Mills must confront the mind of a meticulous and calculating killer. The film explores themes of morality, justice, and the darkness that can exist within human nature.
