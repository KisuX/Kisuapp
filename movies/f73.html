Story

Chris Gardner is a struggling salesman trying to build a better life for himself and his young son. Despite working tirelessly, financial difficulties begin to take over his life, pushing him into a difficult and uncertain situation.

Determined to create a brighter future, Chris takes a risky opportunity by joining an unpaid internship at a prestigious brokerage firm. While competing against many other candidates, he must balance the intense pressure of the program with the responsibility of caring for his child.

Facing hardship, rejection, and moments of doubt, Chris refuses to give up on his dreams. The story highlights perseverance, hope, and the powerful bond between a father and his son as they struggle toward a better future.
