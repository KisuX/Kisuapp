Story

Mümtaz and Derya’s paths cross by chance, and a major accident changes their lives. After the accident, they are forced to wake up in a different body each day, learning about love, family, and life’s surprises through an unexpected cycle.

The film blends comedy and romance, offering the audience both laughter and emotional moments. Filled with humor, emotion, and surprises, this journey is poised to become one of your favorite rom‑coms.

Başka Bir Sen explores the limits of love, expectations, and life’s unforeseen surprises through this extraordinary experience of living in a different body each day, presented in a fun and engaging way. It premiered on Disney+ on March 7, 2025.
