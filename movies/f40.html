Story

Husband-and-wife paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren are called to help the Perron family, who have been experiencing increasingly disturbing events in their Rhode Island farmhouse. As supernatural occurrences intensify, the Warrens discover that the home is plagued by a powerful and malevolent entity.

The investigation tests their faith, expertise, and courage as they confront forces beyond their understanding. The Warrens must uncover the dark history of the house and perform a dangerous exorcism to save the family and themselves.

Filled with tension, jump scares, and a chilling atmosphere, The Conjuring is a masterful blend of real-life inspired horror and cinematic storytelling that explores the battle between light and dark forces.
