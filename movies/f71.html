Story

Truman Burbank lives an ordinary life in the idyllic town of Seahaven, but he is unaware that his entire existence is a carefully orchestrated reality television show broadcast to the world. As he begins to notice inconsistencies and strange events, Truman starts questioning his reality and seeks the truth beyond the constructed world. The film explores themes of privacy, media manipulation, and personal freedom.

Through clever storytelling and suspense, the movie captures Truman’s journey from oblivion to self-awareness, highlighting the human desire for truth and authenticity in a world dominated by illusion.
