Story

In a world where scientists discover a way to transfer human consciousness into realistic robotic animals, animal lover Mabel gets the chance to experience life through the eyes of a beaver. As her mind adapts to this unique form, she embarks on an extraordinary adventure in the animal kingdom, uncovering mysteries and forging unexpected connections across a vibrant and whimsical world.

The film combines heartwarming comedy with adventurous moments as Mabel navigates this new perspective, meeting quirky characters and learning what it truly means to understand creatures great and small.

Filled with imaginative animation and fun set pieces, this family‑friendly story celebrates curiosity, empathy, and the joy of discovery.
