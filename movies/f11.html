Story

In a distant, peaceful future, a young boy named Arco accidentally travels through time and crash‑lands in the year 2075. Lost in an unfamiliar world, he is discovered by a girl his age named Iris, who decides to help him find a way back to his time while navigating the dangers and wonders of her own.

As Arco and Iris grow closer, they embark on a magical adventure filled with friendship, mystery, and discovery. Along the way, they are joined by Iris’s loyal robot guardian, creating a team determined to overcome obstacles and learn the true meaning of courage and belonging.

Arco blends vibrant animation, imaginative sci‑fi world‑building, and touching emotional beats to create a heartwarming story that celebrates imagination, cooperation, and hope in the face of an uncertain future.
