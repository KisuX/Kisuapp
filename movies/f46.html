Story

After his son is brutally kidnapped, Largo Winch dedicates himself to unraveling a conspiracy and uncovering the events that led to financial ruin. In pursuit of the truth and to reunite with his missing son, he embarks on a perilous journey from the forests of Canada to Bangkok, and deep into the heart of Burma.

Set against a backdrop of financial intrigue and personal vengeance, this adventure delivers a globe-spanning chase filled with action and suspense.

The French-Belgian co-production highlights not only high-stakes action across vast locations but also the inner struggles of its characters.
