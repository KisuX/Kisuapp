Story

Set in the early 19th century, Pride and Prejudice follows the lives of the Bennet family as they navigate love, marriage, and societal expectations. Elizabeth Bennet, intelligent and independent, meets the proud Mr. Darcy, and their initial misunderstandings lead to a slow-burning romance shaped by social conventions and personal growth.

The film explores themes of class, family, and personal integrity. Through exquisite cinematography, period costumes, and nuanced performances, it captures the wit, charm, and emotional depth of Jane Austen’s timeless novel.

With memorable characters, stirring music, and elegant visual storytelling, Pride and Prejudice remains a definitive adaptation that balances romance and social commentary in a beautifully realized period drama.
