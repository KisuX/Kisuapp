Story

Forrest Gump, a man with a kind heart and low IQ, recounts his extraordinary life experiences in Alabama. Despite his mental challenges, he influences major historical events, meets presidents, becomes a war hero, and experiences great personal loss and love.

The film combines humor, drama, and romance, exploring themes of destiny, perseverance, and the impact of a single life on the world. Forrest’s unique perspective on life touches all those he meets, making his story both inspiring and heartwarming.

Starring Tom Hanks, Robin Wright, Gary Sinise, and Sally Field, Forrest Gump won six Academy Awards including Best Picture, Best Actor, and Best Director.
