Story

In this family‑friendly adventure, journalist Dan Geraldo travels to the Palombian jungle in search of the elusive Marsupilami, a fantastical creature with a long, prehensile tail. What begins as a simple assignment turns into an unforgettable journey when Geraldo encounters the playful, mysterious animal and becomes wrapped up in an environmental struggle to protect its home.

As Geraldo learns more about the values of friendship, loyalty, and conservation, he finds himself in the middle of a conflict between greedy hunters and the creatures of the jungle. With the help of a guide and the Marsupilami’s antics, the group navigates dangers both humorous and thrilling.

Marsupilami blends slapstick comedy, adventurous set pieces, and heartfelt moments to create a lively tale that both children and adults can enjoy, all set against the lush backdrop of the jungle.
