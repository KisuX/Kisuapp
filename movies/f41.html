Story

Şakir and his friends set out to stop a music group controlled by artificial intelligence from hypnotizing the city. The strange hypnotic state in the city propels them into an adventure across multiple worlds.

Alongside Mirket, they track the servers controlling the power of music, facing challenges in diverse settings like an amusement park, a subway station, and the Basilica Cistern. In each new world, they confront obstacles to thwart the plan.

Filled with fun characters and colorful worlds, this animated adventure emphasizes friendship, teamwork, and courage, offering a family-friendly experience for all ages.
