Story

Bi Umut is based on a true story. Gülsüm Kabadayı, who lives in Antalya, accidentally encounters a young man paralyzed in a traffic accident whose identity cannot be determined. Doctors say he “has only three months to live,” but thanks to Gülsüm’s love and dedication, he clings to life. The film tells how this woman not only becomes a mother to Umut but also demonstrates the power of love, hope, and solidarity despite life’s harsh challenges.

Gülsüm’s decade-long devotion to Umut highlights the human capacity for kindness alongside themes of loneliness and helplessness. Built around family, sacrifice, and hope, the film aims to evoke deep emotions in the audience.

The film premiered in theaters on November 7, 2025, featuring a broad cast including Hülya Duyar, Leon Kemstach, and Celal Öztürk.
