Story

A journalist, Rachel Keller, investigates a mysterious videotape which seems to cause the death of anyone who watches it seven days later. Racing against time, she tries to uncover the origins of the tape and stop the deadly curse while confronting dark secrets of the past.

The film combines supernatural horror with suspense, exploring themes of curiosity, fear, and the consequences of uncovering hidden truths.

Starring Naomi Watts, Martin Henderson, Brian Cox, and David Dorfman, The Ring became a modern horror classic and spawned multiple sequels and adaptations.
