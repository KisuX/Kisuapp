Story

Henry Roth, a marine veterinarian in Hawaii, falls for Lucy Whitmore, a woman suffering from short-term memory loss after a car accident. Every day she forgets him, forcing Henry to win her heart anew each morning.

The film combines romantic comedy with heartfelt drama, exploring love, patience, and creativity in overcoming personal obstacles. Henry’s relentless efforts highlight the power of devotion and understanding.

Starring Adam Sandler and Drew Barrymore, 50 First Dates is remembered for its humor, charm, and the chemistry between the leads, making it a classic romantic comedy of the 2000s.
