Story

The film retells the legendary tale of the Trojan War, focusing on the conflict sparked by Paris, Prince of Troy, taking Helen, the wife of Spartan King Menelaus. Warriors and leaders from both sides clash, highlighting heroism, betrayal, and the cost of honor and glory. Themes of love, revenge, and destiny drive the epic narrative as Troy faces its ultimate fate.

Troy combines large-scale battles, intricate politics, and personal stories of iconic characters like Achilles, Hector, and Helen, delivering a cinematic portrayal of one of history’s most famous wars.
