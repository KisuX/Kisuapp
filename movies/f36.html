Story

Nelson Moss is a workaholic advertising executive who meets the free-spirited Sara Deever, a woman who invites him to spend a transformative month together in November. Sara’s unconventional approach to life teaches Nelson to appreciate love, happiness, and living in the moment. Their brief but intense relationship challenges both characters to confront personal fears and emotional barriers.

Sweet November explores themes of love, personal growth, and the fleeting nature of time, showing how one meaningful month can leave a lasting impact on a person’s life.

The film blends heartfelt romance with emotional depth, offering a story of connection, change, and the power of opening one’s heart.
