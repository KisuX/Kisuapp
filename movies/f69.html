Story

Two strangers wake up in a dilapidated bathroom, chained at opposite ends, and discover they are pawns in a deadly game orchestrated by the mysterious Jigsaw. Each must confront personal choices and solve lethal puzzles to survive. The film explores psychological terror, moral dilemmas, and the lengths people go to stay alive.

Saw is the first installment in a long-running horror franchise, known for its suspenseful storytelling and iconic plot twists. The movie emphasizes strategy, fear, and human desperation.
