Story

Leon is a man living alone in New York, known for his cold-blooded efficiency as a professional hitman. One day, he encounters 12-year-old Mathilda, whose family has been killed by the mafia, and takes her into his apartment. Initially distant, Leon develops a protective and genuine bond with Mathilda, a relationship that profoundly changes both of their lives.

As Mathilda learns survival skills and the path to revenge from Leon, their connection deepens. The film explores themes of friendship, loneliness, loyalty, and the meaning of being human with dramatic intensity.

Written and directed by Luc Besson, this work is regarded as a classic in cinematic history, praised for its powerful performances and compelling story.
