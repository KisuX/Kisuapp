Story

After a long time apart, Nergis discovers a letter and an old photograph among her deceased mother’s belongings. The person in the photo turns out to be a sibling she never knew existed. To solve this mystery, Nergis travels to Büyükada and decides to investigate her mother’s former workplace, the Greek Orphanage, and the traces of her sibling’s disappearance.

During her stay at the orphanage, Nergis witnesses frightening events and dark secrets from the past. As she encounters malevolent spirits and ghostly figures, her desire to escape the island grows, and the situation becomes increasingly dangerous.

The film combines horror and mystery elements with the unique atmosphere of Büyükada, delivering an engaging and suspenseful story to the audience.
