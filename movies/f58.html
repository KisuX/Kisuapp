Story

The film tells the story of a family whose journey in search of peace turns into a nightmare. As ancient secrets hidden in an old seal and forbidden rituals come to light, dark forces, including jinn and spells, begin to threaten those around them. While confronting their inner fears, the family is drawn into these supernatural powers.

The movie immerses the audience in a chilling atmosphere with psychological and metaphysical horror elements revolving around magic, jinn, and dark secrets. Curiosity and fear intertwine as the characters face both their own pasts and the terror of the unknown.

"Şeyatin‑i Cin" blends supernatural horror with Turkish horror cinema, creating a dark mythology and promising viewers an unforgettable frightening experience.
