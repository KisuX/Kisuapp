Story

Riley is growing up, and with her teenage years approaching, new emotions begin to appear inside her mind. Joy, Sadness, Anger, Fear, and Disgust suddenly find themselves dealing with unexpected changes in the emotional headquarters.

As Riley faces new challenges in her life, unfamiliar feelings begin to influence her thoughts and decisions. The emotions must learn how to work together once again while trying to understand these new emotional experiences.

Through humor, heart, and imaginative storytelling, the film explores the complexity of growing up and the importance of every emotion. Inside Out 2 continues the colorful journey inside Riley’s mind, showing how feelings shape who we become.
