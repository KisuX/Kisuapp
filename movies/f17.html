Story

After a paragliding accident leaves wealthy aristocrat Philippe paralyzed, he hires Driss, a young man recently released from prison, to be his caregiver. Although Driss has no experience, Philippe gives him a chance, and their unlikely friendship transforms both their lives as they discover joy, trust, and connection in unexpected ways.

Set against the backdrop of class divides and cultural contrasts, the film blends heartfelt drama with gentle humor. It’s a moving and inspiring story about how two very different people can form a deep and life‑changing bond.

Celebrated worldwide and acclaimed for its performances—especially Omar Sy’s—*The Intouchables* remains one of the most beloved films about friendship and human resilience.
