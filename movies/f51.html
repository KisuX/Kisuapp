Story

In 1975, under the shadow of the economic crisis caused by the American embargo and political turmoil, Yeşilçam cinema underwent a transformation. Aytekin, a theater actor, tries to stay away from the surge of erotic films while holding on to his ideals, but the responsibility of his sick wife and politically involved son inadvertently pulls him into this new world. Aytekin's efforts to protect his family, after achieving unexpected fame, further complicate his life.

The film portrays the tragic story of a man caught between his passion for art, family responsibilities, and personal honor, examining the transformation of Turkish cinema in the 1970s through both humorous and dramatic lenses.

Parçalı Yıllar offers a look at a forgotten era of Turkish cinema, bringing to the screen the clash between individual ideals and social realities.
